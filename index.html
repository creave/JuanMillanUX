About

My keen eye for sophisticated aesthetics and deep understanding of human behavior allows me to build products that are beautiful and highly functional. All while keeping business goals in mind. Listen, I’ve been doing this for over 17 years. During that time I’ve been involved in all the steps in the Project Life Cycle.

I have experience as a BA (Business Analyst) and writing the BRD (Business Requirements Document). I can take those BRD to build wireframes, user flows, mockups and prototypes. I also have experience managing projects in both waterfall and agile methodologies. When it comes to project management I provide more value when my role is the project owner.

But enough about me. What about you?
I’m always looking for the next project that will use technology to make people’s life more productive. Look forward hearing from you soon and build something amazing.
